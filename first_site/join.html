How to buy an EntertainmentTM Book

All you need to be able to take advantage of the great deals at various restaurants in your city, is to support you favourity charity by buying an Entertainment Book.

The new Entertainment™ Books are better than ever and are packed with thousands of valuable offers from popular restaurants, attractions, accommodation and more! Purchase a new Book and you will also be helping community organisations with their fundraising, with 20% of the proceeds going directly towards their important causes.

Entertainment™ Books are distributed exclusively through a number of fund-raising and community groups in your local area such as:

  • Charities
  • Schools
  • Hospitals
  • Corporate Social Clubs
  • Rotary Clubs and Lions Clubs
  • Sporting Clubs
  • Day Care Centres and Kindergartens
  • Religious Groups
  • Research Foundations
  • Scout and Girl Guide Groups
  • Surf Lifesaving Clubs
  • Local Community Services Groups

Choose an organisation in your local area that you would like to support… your favourite charity, sporting club, the local school or hospital and give them a call or visit their website.

Many organisations are already taking orders for the new 2013|2014 Entertainment™ Books that will be released from April 2013 onwards so get in touch with your favourite fundraising organisation now to reserve your new Entertainment™ Book.
